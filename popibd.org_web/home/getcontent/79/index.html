Core Competence

POPI works over last twenty five years addressed broad issues of social mobilization, community empowerment, human resource and institutional development alongside with resource mobilization through an extensive partnership and networking to support for causes of our program participants. It achieves this through unique strengths in fund management, program design, implementation and monitoring together with a broad range of project and program management and community development competence.

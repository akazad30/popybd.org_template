Our Vision

POPI envisions a prudent nation, free from hunger and poverty where every citizen leads a life with dignity and equity.

Our Mission

To achieve the vision, POPI is striving with the following missions:

To work for the people and with the people irrespective of religion, caste, ethnicity, creed, belief age and sex who are in any form of marginalization and exclusion by addressing root causes of their marginalization, exclusion, inhuman situation and exploitation for ensuring security, justice and dignified living. To implement appropriate, need based, result oriented development and welfare programs and projects that enhance participation of targeted people to bring significant positive changes in their lives and living.

To build linkages and render cooperation/coordination with among government, civil societies, NGOs, and community based organization for best utilization of scarce resources in attaining sustainable development for the targeted people. To build and enhance capacity, competence and commitments of personnel, institutions and groups who are involved in development and welfare activities. To institutionalize the system and procedures that facilitates the growth of POPI as sustainable organization.

Our Objectives

  • Strengthening unity and building resilient rural and urban communities of the poor and the marginalized.
  • Food and livelihood security for the individual poor and marginalized family.
  • Human resource development through education and health hygiene promotion and capacity building in transaction able professional skill.
  • Promotion of growth and economic self reliance of the poor through holistic development approach to address root causes of poverty and marginalization.
  • Agriculture development maintaining bio diversity and environment protection.
  • Community based Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR).
  • Prevention of discrimination and violence against women and the weak and increase equity and dignity of women and the under privileged.
  • Promotion of homestead, institution, private and public area sanitation.
  • Promotion of safe water facilities for the poor and the marginalized.
  • Establishment of basic human rights and constitutional rights of the country.
  • Establishment linkage with regional national and global networks and forum for pro poor or poverty alleviation issues campaign.

Approach

POPI follows mainly interlinked and mixed approaches based on nature and needs of its programme participants. These are:

  • Community led, holistic development approach
  • Participatory approach
  • Rights based approach
  • Partnership approach
  • Responsible wellbeing approach

Targeted Population

  • Most Marginalized Citizen (MMC)/ Ultra Poor/ Extreme Poor, Dalit Families, Children, Men and Women
  • Socially excluded women (Sex Workers, Victims of extortion, rape, abuse etc.)
  • Ethnic Minority Poor (Children, Adolescent Girls, Men and Women)
  • Orphan Children, Street Children, and Working Children
  • Disabled people
  • Marginal and small farmers, fisherman, small traders etc.
  • Vulnerable to river erosion, Haor and adverse geographical condition

Strategic Program Priorities (2011 - 2015)

In order to implement development projects and program, POPI supplement and complement the current national development priorities of the GoB, it is explicitly focused to MDG through Bangladesh Sixth Five Year Plan, 2011-2015: Accelerated Growth and Reducing Poverty and their targets. POPI also follow CRC and ILO Convention 182 values as its guiding principles which influences the way each child’s rights is fulfilled, and serve as a constant reference for the implementation and monitoring of all efforts to fulfill and protect children’s rights. POPI’s current sector wise strategic programme priorities are as follows:


Education:
Continue and scale-up Early Child Care & Development program. Participate in social movement to increase access of poor children to education. Mobilize Parents, CBO/VDC and civil society to improve quality of education. Special focus on girls’ education, drop-out children because of poverty, education for disable & vocational education for adolescent boys & girls.


Health, Nutrition & WASH:
Facilitate awareness, skills and practices on health, hygiene and nutrition. Reduce health risk and malnutrition of mother, children & adolescents, initiate program to reduce HIV/AIDS transmission. Net-work with GoB health services and actively participate movement for improvement, access and availability of health services. Contribute to vision-2021 through awareness & skills, disaster resilient infrastructure and access to WASH.

Rights & Governance:
Building capacity of CBO/VDC to create access to decision making process. Building network of CBO/VDC with UP and facilitate collective work.  Work through active participation of the community, NGO and other actors to strengthen LGIs, to enhance its transparency and accountability of duty bearers and ensuring access to information.
Act as an active member to all kind of rights promotion movement for women, children, ethnic, haor & char people. Advocacy to sensitize duty bearers and create greater awareness on human rights issues. Organize and mobilize communities to eradicate especially worst forms of Child labor, gender and social discrimination. Facilitating the process for access and control over natural/common resources.

Alternative Livelihood Security Options:
Explore On-farm, Off-Farm income generating options for poor, women and seasonal unemployed. Facilitate skills development support, diversified use of local resources and capitalization of local resource persons. Building livelihood resilience through asset transfer in cash and kind and technical support. Promote entrepreneurs using agricultural and low-cost input and provide support for market linkage. Facilitating the process for access to social safety-net and other services.

Disaster Risk Reduction and Climate Change Adaptation:
Building disaster resilient community, facilitate adaptation to Climate Change, LGIs. Provide support to reduce hardship and shock of natural disaster and climate change consequences. Facilitate and participate all sorts of local/regional disaster and climate change preparedness, response, rehabilitation and mitigation program and advocacy campaign.

Organizational Sustainability:
Strengthening capacity, Advocacy, Net-working, further improvement of policies & services, documentation, improved system and program quality. Explore multi-dimensional income generating options.

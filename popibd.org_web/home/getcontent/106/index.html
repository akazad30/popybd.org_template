DREAM

Project Title : Development and Rehabilitation of Extreme Poor through Alternative Means
Working Area : Upazela : Lishoreganj Sadar & Nikli, District: Kishoreganj
Beneficiaries : 10000 HH
Major Components :
  1. Health & Wellbeing
  2. Capacity building
  3. Voice raising
  4. Infrastructure
  5. Alternative Livelihood
  6. Staff development
Funded by : Cordaid – Netherlands

Project Goal:

The most marginalized citizens of project areas lead a secured and dignified life free from hunger and poverty.

 

Specific Objectives:

  • To strengthen access of government quality health care services, to provide primary health care and reproductive health care services to the poor and MMCs living at remote difficult areas (thus enhanced health MDG for Bangladesh).
  • To improve socio-economic empowerment of MMC households so that they can enjoy self well being by own decision and means.
  • To strengthen MMC civil organizations (VDC, MMC Federation, Green Bank Cell etc.) to establish rights in government entitlements, government resources and access to justice.
  • To improve IGA options of MMC to attain livelihood security.
  • To enhance capacity of project staff and organization in facilitating the process of MMCs empowerment and promotion of rights.

Micro-Finance Service Delivery Program

Program Goal:

Achieving economic empowerment of the People of underprivileged section. 

Major Outcomes of the Program:

Food security, livelihood security, safe living and economic sovereignty of women in real sense.

Service Area

Rangpur Division : Lalmonirhat, Nilphamari, Dinajpur, Rangpur.

Dhaka Division : Mymensingh, Netrokona, Kishoreganj, Gazipur, Narshingdi, Dhaka, Narayanganj, Munshiganj.

Chittagong Division : Brahmanbaria, Comilla, Chittagong, Cox’s Bazar.

Major Features at a Glance

-          Group formation

-          Savings fund (General savings, Special savings, Secure Future Fund)

-          Rural Micro Credit

-          Urban Micro Credit

-          Micro-enterprise

-          Training and capacity building,

-          Provide technical assistance and marketing facilities

-          Awareness building on different social issue

-          Loan coverage in emergency

-          Participatory livestock development

-          Agricultural Micro Credit

-          Micro Insurance for Mutual Enabling.

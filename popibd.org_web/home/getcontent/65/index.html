Genesis & Growth

Starting from over two decades ago in 1986 at a village Jamalpur (under Bhairab Upazela, Kishoreganj district) with tireless efforts of prospective Youths, POPI is continuing development activities with same commitment and determination as it has applied to the mission since inception. The principles of objectivity, built on fairness, efficiency and effectiveness, which were adopted since the beginning, have remained immutable in the face of all the challenges that the organization had encountered. Trustworthy is one of the central tenets of POPI’s beliefs. These core values that POPI is fostering over the years. Trials and learning are essential components of POPI’s approach strategies while remaining firmly committed to its mission. It is equipped and able to adapt to the changing global and national development needs and environment by holding dynamic flexibility to accept and admire perceptions of new needs, new situation and expectations. It has determination to be sensitive and responsive to humanity and human crisis alleviation, work shoulder to shoulder with people who endeavors to bring changes and complement national efforts of changes through pursuing reduction of poverty, causes of poverty, addressing and upliftment of human rights for the most disadvantaged people.

By dint of perseverance, hard and prudent endeavors, POPI raised itself to the status of national level NGO within a time period of 10 years and has kept the growth steady and glamorous among people particularly for improving status of the Poor, Ultra Poor and disadvantaged groups of people in Bangladesh. Presently the organisation holds a commendable position of working at more than 113 Upazelas in 19 Districts under 4 Divisions of the country. All its development programmes are implemented through community approach by nearly 3000 well trained regular staff and more than 1500 paid volunteers. POPI loves and desires to be a true people’s entity with the principles of democracy, good governance, modern thinking and human values.

Core Competence

POPI works over last twenty five years addressed broad issues of social mobilization, community empowerment, human resource and institutional development alongside with resource mobilization through an extensive partnership and networking to support for causes of our program participants. It achieves this through unique strengths in fund management, program design, implementation and monitoring together with a broad range of project and program management and community development competence.

Values, POPI Mainatains

  • Respectful behavior with all people and the value of diversity
  • Honesty, sincerity, transparency and accountability to all levels for the effectiveness
  • Equity and justice
  • Humanity and perseverance
  • Solidarity with the poor, powerless and excluded
  • Independence from any religious or political affiliation

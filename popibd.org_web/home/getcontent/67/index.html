Governance

POPI has an Executive Committee consisting 7 members who are elected by the General Council members. The General Council comprises of 21 members from the community. The Executive Committee is responsible for guiding and watching the activities of the organization. This committee develops policy guideline at the organizational level. Besides the General Council and Executive Committee, there is also another team that includes all departmental heads which includes Executive Director and Directors of the units. They are supported by Programme Managers and Coordinators who are responsible to carry out all the activities of the individual projects with the assistance of respective technical programme staff.

POPI fulfills all the standard rules and regulations set by Bangladesh Government which includes approved Constitution, Personnel Policy, Accounting Manual and Procurement Policy, Micro-credit Policy, Strategic Plan for the organization etc. Presently it covers more than four million poor and marginalized extreme poor population with welfare, development and right based services deliveries at 5659 villages of 805 unions under 113 Upazelas in 19 district of Dhaka, Chittagang, Sylhet and Rangpur divisions of Bangladesh. It runs program and project activities through 229 offices and by a team of around 3000 qualified and experienced regular staff.

Legal Status

POPI is legally registered with:

  • Department of Social Welfare, under voluntary social welfare organisation (Registration and control), Ordinance 1961 Sec-46. Registration No: Kishore/0056, dated 15.11.1986;
  • NGO affairs Bureau. Reg. No: 507, dated 30.07.1991 under Foreign Donation (Voluntary activities0 Regulation Ordinance (Ordinance 46, 1978);
  • Micro Credit Regulatory Authority act 2006 [Sec-16, sub-section- 3] Registration # 02150-01563-01159   dated 16 march, 2008.
